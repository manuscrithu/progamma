samurai kenji

directions..

  • This game is a multiplayer game. Two players are able to play at a time.
  • Left player is the samurai player. He can be controlled by A, W, S, D keys and keys for move left, jump, attack and move right respectfully.
  • Right side player is the Kenji. He can be controlled by ArrowLeft, ArrowUp, ArrowDown, ArrowRight keys and keys for move left, jump, attack and move right respectfully.

  • KeyFunction
    AMove left (Samurai)
    WJump (Samurai)
    SAttack (Samurai)
    DMove right (Samurai)
    ArrowLeftMove left (Kenji)
    ArrowUpJump (Kenji)
    ArrowDownAttack (Kenji)
    ArrowRightMove right (Kenji)

  • Each player can take maximum 5 hits. After 5 hits the player who takes hit will be lose.
  • Time duration is 60 seconds. Within 60 seconds player should play. If no player loses, winner will be determined according to the health level at the end of the 60 seconds.

Enjoy then..
